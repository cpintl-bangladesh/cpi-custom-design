## 3. LOGO SPECIFICATIONS

### 3.1 Logo Variants

| Variant           | Usage                                     |
| ----------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Primary Logo      | Default use in all external materials     |
| Primary + Tagline | Official letterheads & stationery         |
| Secondary Logo    | Space-limited contexts (icon size)        |
| White (reversed)  | On dark/colored backgrounds (purple, red) |

### 3.2 Clear Space Rule

Minimum clear space = width of the letter "P" in the CPI wordmark, on all four sides.

### 3.3 Minimum Sizes

| Variant        | Minimum Height |
| -------------- | -------------- |
| Primary Logo   | 0.5 cm         |
| Secondary Logo | 0.9 cm         |
| Logo + Tagline | 0.8 cm         |

### 3.4 Logo Placement (on page)

Permitted positions: Top Left, Top Right, Bottom Left, Bottom Right.

### 3.5 Logo DON'Ts (Never Do)

* Never change logo proportions or distort
* Never rotate the logo
* Never recolor the logo elements
* Never place on busy photographic background
* Never place on non-approved background colors
* Never use below minimum size
* Never change the typeface in the wordmark

## 5. DESIGN ELEMENTS

### 5.1 Key Shapes

1. Rectangle with rounded top corners — overlay on images
2. Title + image in key shape — for featured content
3. Image in rounded key shape (left or right) — editorial layouts
4. CPI Red stroke line + rounded photo — report/story layouts

### 5.2 Watermark Rules

* Position: **Right side only**
* Color: Grey on white background (46% opacity)
* Dark version: Full opacity grey on multiply (on CPI background colors)

### 5.3 Color Overlay on Images

CPI Red or any primary color at 40–60% opacity over photos for branded section headers.

### 5.4 Icon Style

* Solid fill (on white background)
* Non-solid fill / outline (on colored backgrounds)
* Color: CPI Red or White depending on background

---

## 6. PHOTOGRAPHY PRINCIPLES

* Show communities as  **active agents** , not passive recipients
* Depict **vibrant, diverse** communities engaged in solutions
* Avoid images that frame beneficiaries as helpless
* Use real, contextual photography from Cox's Bazar / Myanmar programs
* Photo overlay: CPI Red at 40–60% opacity for branded headers

---

## 7. TONE OF VOICE

* **Empowering** : Respect community agency and equal partnership
* **Inspiring** : Give hope, show impact, connect supporter to change
* **Inclusive & Respectful** : Non-discriminatory, diverse, empathetic
* **Accessible** : Plain language, no jargon, open to all audiences
* **Appropriate** : Match tone to context (formal donor report vs. community brief)

---

## 8. CO-BRANDING RULES (with YPSA / others)

* Use CPI Primary Logo (without tagline) in co-branded logo arrays
* Give equal visual weight to all partner logos
* Maintain clear space between logos
* Use white background for logo bars
* Never blend or merge logos
