# Zarish Offline Form Theme

**Optimized for low-literacy, field-use clinical forms**

---

| Role          | Color     | Hex         | Notes                          |
| ------------- | --------- | ----------- | ------------------------------ |
| Background    | White     | `#FFFFFF` | Max readability                |
| Section head  | ZH Teal   | `#00555A` | Clear section delineation      |
| Field bg      | Warm gray | `#F7F9EC` | Distinguishes input from label |
| Required star | Danger    | `#FF006E` | Clear required indicator       |
| Success tick  | Green     | `#38A169` | Validated field indicator      |
| Text          | Gun Metal | `#00272C` | High contrast body text        |

 **Typography** : Noto Sans Bengali (primary for bilingual forms), Inter (English labels)
 **Use when** : Patient registration forms, CHW data entry, follow-up records
 **Rule** : Always bilingual (Bengali primary, English secondary). Large touch targets (min 44px).
